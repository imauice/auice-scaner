Policy privacy

  • 1. Information Collection and Use:
    • - The barcode scanner app is designed to scan and decode barcodes, and it does not collect or store any personal information from users.
    • - The app does not require users to provide any personal details or permissions unrelated to the scanning functionality.
  • 2. Camera Permissions:
    • - The app requires access to the device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning barcodes.
    • - The camera is only accessed when the user initiates a barcode scan.
    • - No images or video captured by the camera are stored or transmitted by the app.
  • 3. Storage:
    • - The app may temporarily store scanned barcode data locally on the device for the purpose of processing and displaying the scanned information to the user.
    • - The stored barcode data is automatically deleted from the device once the user closes the app.
  • 4. Third-Party Services:
    • - The app may utilize third-party barcode scanning libraries or APIs to enhance the scanning functionality.
    • - These services may have their own privacy policies, and the user's interaction with them is subject to their respective terms and conditions.
    • - However, the app does not share any user data or personally identifiable information with these third-party services.
  • 5. Data Sharing:
    • - The barcode scanner app does not share any scanned barcode data with third parties, including advertisers or marketers.
    • - The app does not engage in any form of data selling, monetization, or targeted advertising.
  • 6. Security:
    • - The app employs industry-standard security measures to protect scanned barcode data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.
    • - Regular security updates are implemented to address any potential vulnerabilities.
  • 7. Children's Privacy:
    • - The barcode scanner app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.
    • - If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided any personal information through the app, they should contact the app developer to have it removed.
  • 8. Changes to the Privacy Policy:
    • - Any changes to the privacy policy will be communicated to users through app updates or notifications.
    • - Users are encouraged to review the privacy policy periodically for any updates or modifications.
  • 9. Contact Information:
    • - Users can contact the app developer for any inquiries, concerns, or requests related to privacy or the app's functionality.
